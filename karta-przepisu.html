Creamy Tomato Pasta

Classic comfort food that's ready in under 30 minutes. A perfect weeknight meal the whole family will love.

arrow_back Back to Recipes

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions. Drain, reserving 1 cup of pasta water.
  2. While pasta is cooking, melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Stir in tomato paste and cook for 1 minute more. Pour in heavy cream and bring to a simmer. Reduce heat and let it bubble gently for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Stir in grated Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth. Season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes, if using.
  5. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to coat. If the sauce is too thick, add a splash of the reserved pasta water until it reaches your desired consistency.
  6. Garnish with fresh basil and serve immediately.

Prep & Cook Time

Prep Time
10 mins
Cook Time
20 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pasta (like fettuccine or penne)
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup tomato paste
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Fresh basil, for garnish
